## Foodie_Express - Restaurant Management System

**Welcome to Foodie_Express, your one-stop solution for managing your restaurant operations seamlessly.**

**Product Showcase:**

Upon entering the Foodie_Express web application, you'll be greeted with a captivating display of four random products, each paired with its corresponding menu category. This dynamic section will keep your customers engaged and entice them to explore your culinary offerings.

**Top Sellers:**

Next, you'll find a curated selection of four top-selling products, highlighting your restaurant's most popular dishes. This section serves as a testament to your culinary expertise and guides customers towards their next favorite meal.

**Discounted Delights:**

To further entice your customers, Foodie_Express features a section dedicated to discounted products. These three items, carefully selected based on their sales history, are offered at enticing discounts, attracting customers seeking value for their money.

**Table Reservations:**

Foodie_Express simplifies the table reservation process, allowing customers to secure their preferred dining spot with ease. The system intelligently assigns tables based on available seating, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable dining experience for all.

**Shopping Cart and Billing:**

As customers browse your delectable offerings, they can easily add items to their shopping cart. Once ready, they can proceed to checkout, where they'll have the option to choose between dine-in or delivery.

**Dine-in:**

For dine-in customers, Foodie_Express generates a convenient PDF bill, providing a clear and organized breakdown of their order. This feature streamlines the billing process and enhances the dining experience.

**Delivery:**

For delivery orders, customers will be prompted to enter their address details. The system will then assign the order to an available delivery driver, ensuring timely and efficient delivery service.

**User-Friendly Experience:**

Foodie_Express eliminates the need for repetitive logins, allowing customers to enjoy a seamless and uninterrupted experience. You, as the restaurant owner, maintain control over the application, ensuring that your culinary creations are always at the forefront.

**Embark on a Culinary Journey with Foodie_Express**

Embrace the power of Foodie_Express and transform your restaurant's online presence. With its intuitive interface and user-centric features, Foodie_Express will elevate your restaurant to new heights of success.
